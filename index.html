AYIEP is a visionary initiative by the Ministry of Sports and Youth Development, Abia State aimed at shaping a comprehensive and inclusive Youth Development Framework.

Our Mission

To create a dynamic and inclusive state government youth development, empowerment, and innovation program that nurtures our youth’s potential, employs data-driven techniques and promotes innovation across several domains such as sports ,technology and health.

Our Mission

To build an inclusive state where every young person is supported to thrive, actively contribute to society and realize their full potential.
