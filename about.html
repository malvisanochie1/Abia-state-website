About

Who are we

We are thrilled to introduce an initiative aimed at elevating and empowering  Abia state’s dynamic youth. Our goal is to develop leadership in the next generation of young people by providing opportunities and encouraging creativity.  

This program will focus on data-driven youth development processes, talent harvesting in sports, access to information, business and enterprise, incubation through the innovation lab program, capacity development, and empowerment monitoring to create a robust framework that addresses the multifaceted needs of our young population while also focusing on addressing critical issues such as sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHR) and drug abuse. AYEIP is designed to promote inclusion, upskilling, technology integration, and sports excellence while driving innovation, collaboration, and economic growth among the youth population of the state.

The goal of AYEIP is to help young people reach their full potential by giving them access to a range of resources, support networks and programs.

My holistic vision for the Abia State, Youth & Sports Sector is to drive a sustainable and Inclusive youth development and promote sports excellence as a contributor to economic growth, healthy living and a safe society.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Mission

To create a dynamic and inclusive state government youth development, empowerment, and innovation program that nurtures our youth’s potential, employs data-driven techniques and promotes innovation across several domains such as sports ,technology and health.

Key Strategies

Youth Engagement

Involve youths in the design, implementation, and evaluation of AYEIP to ensure it meets their needs and aspirations.Conduct extensive outreach and engagement efforts to involve as many youth as possible through schools, community centers,and online platforms.

Public-Private Partnership

Collaborate with businesses,educational institutions, and NGOs to leverage resources,expertise,and networks for maximum impact.

Digital Infrastructure

Leverage digital tools and platforms to facilitate information access, data collection, and program monitoring and also program applications, reporting,and feedback to streamline program management and communication.

Evaluation and Adaptation

Regularly assess the program's effectiveness through impact assessments, surveys, and feedback mechanisms, and adjust strategies accordingly.

Resource Mobilization

Secure funding from a combination of government budgets, grants, private sector investments, and international development partners.

Sustainability

Develop a long-term sustainability plan that includes securing funding, building local capacity, and institutionalizing the program within state government structures
